the beginning: twilight forest + artifacts

so this modpack doesn't have astral sorcery, but it does have some mod interactions that are very powerful.

in particular, this modpack allows artifacts campsites to spawn in the twilight forest, on the surface where they're easily visible. therefore, your first step should be to rush entering the twilight forest. to do that, you'll need a diamond, some water, and some flowers.
