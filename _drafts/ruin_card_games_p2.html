one good way to ruin a card game is by cheating.

cheating can be sorted into two categories: discreet and blatant. these two types work differently and do different things.

1a: discreet cheating

discreet cheating is cheating where you try to not be noticed. it can have several goals:

  • giving you an advantage in the game. this makes other players win less and maybe enjoy the game less. one example of this is surreptitiously grabbing free coins or victory points, or stacking the deck.
  • giving someone else a disadvantage in the game. i'll cover this in more detail in a later part, but one example is peeking at someone's card before you tell them that they're bleeding.
